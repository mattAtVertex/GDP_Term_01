# Assignment 02: Create Landscape and Sky Atmosphere

<p>In this assignment, you will build a Landscape. I encourage you to use reference and to pick a landscape the is inspired by reality. For instance, choose a particular landscape on earth and try to mimic it as much as possible. Make sure to gather plenty of photo reference to help you. You will also want to make note of the biome of your landscape and plan accordingly for foliage.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>You will use Gaea to create the heightfield, then create a custom landscape material for painting and blending various materials on your landscape.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We will then use the sky atmosphere system and atmospheric height fog to round out the environment.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As your final step, you will make use of some assets from Quixel using Quixel Bridge to add foliage to your landscape.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Once your environment is complete, you will take up to 5 high resolution screenshots of your landscape and submit them for review.&nbsp;</p>
